Pilates

Pilates is a form of exercise, developed by Joseph Pilates, which emphasises the balanced development of the body through core strength, flexibility, and awareness in order to support efficient and graceful movement. Pilates works by combining breathing techniques with special active and passive stretches combined with movement.
